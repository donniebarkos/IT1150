Who Are We?

Beanie Beaches is a project run by a small, committed team of long-time Beanie Baby collectors.
What started as a way to trade Beanies and manage our collections has blossomed into a community of buyers and sellers,
ran by and for Beanie fans. Beanie Beaches works as a middle man for Beanie Bids, facilitating the purchasing
and shipment of Beanies around the world. Like Ebay...but cuddlier!
